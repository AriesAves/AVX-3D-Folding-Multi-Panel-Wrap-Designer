Fold Allowance (creep · paper-thickness compensation)
0.060 in · 1.5 mm
Each consecutive panel with the same fold direction as the previous one is narrowed by this amount so the stacked layers nest without binding (~1–3 mm typical). The total finished document width is preserved by redistributing the difference to the first panel of the run.
